### 1. Asgardeo Authentication Setup

You will be guided through a 3-step setup:

1. **Enter Organization Name**
   - This is your Asgardeo organization (e.g., `org123`).
2. **Enter Client ID**
   - Create a Public Client in Asgardeo Console.
   - Enter the **Client ID**.
3. **Sign in with Asgardeo**
   - You will be redirected to Asgardeo to authenticate.
   - On success, you are returned and signed in.
